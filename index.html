App Store Screenshots Generator

Tired of generating your own App Store screenshots?
Upload 1 to 5 images at once, and we will convert all your images for you, ready to be uploaded.



UPLOAD

The Apple App Store accepts 1 - 5 images as screenshots for your application. Upload all of them here and watch the magic happen!

Ensure your uploaded file dimensions are either from iPhone 5 or 6 (either one of 2 - 4).


CONVERT

A total of four screenshot sizes are required, and we generate all of them for you (px):

  1. 640 x 960 (iPhone 3/4)
  2. 640 x 1136 (iPhone 5/5S)
  3. 750 x 1334 (iPhone 6/6S)
  4. 1242 x 2208 (iPhone 6 Plus)


SUBMIT TO APPSTORE

We generate and package your screenshots in folders which are ready for upload to the App Store.

Simply drag all of the screenshots in each folder to the relevant sections in iTunesConnect.
Before you can convert your screenshots, you need to have a set of them. Here's how you do it:

  1. Run your application on the iPhone Simulator (iPhone 5 / 6 / 6 Plus).
  2. Under the Window tab, scale to 100%. This is important as it ensures proper dimensions and better resolution.
  3. Hit Command + S to take a screenshot.
  4. Upload to appstorescreenshots.com to convert to all other required sizes.
